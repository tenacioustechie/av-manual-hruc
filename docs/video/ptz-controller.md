# PTZ Controller

The **AVKANS Joy Pro Controller** is the joystick box used to **move the
cameras by hand**. Use it when you need a shot that has not been saved as a
preset. For everyday switching and presets, the [StreamDeck](../presentation/streamdeck-buttons.md)
is easier.

📷 *Screenshot placeholder: AVKANS Joy Pro Controller with joystick, zoom rocker and camera-select buttons labelled.*

---

## What the main parts do

### The joystick
Moving the joystick **pans and tilts** the selected camera:

- Push **left / right** → the camera turns left / right (**pan**).
- Push **up / down** → the camera looks up / down (**tilt**).
- Push gently → it moves **slowly**. Push further → it moves **faster**.

### The zoom control
A rocker or twist control **zooms** the selected camera:

- One way zooms **in** (closer / tighter).
- The other way zooms **out** (wider).

### The camera select buttons
Buttons that choose **which camera** the joystick controls — **Camera 1** or
**Camera 2**. The joystick only moves the camera you have selected.

### The preset buttons
Number buttons that **save** and **recall** camera positions (used together
with a "set"/"store" button). These match the presets you can also reach from
the StreamDeck.

---

## Before you move a camera

!!! warning "Only move a camera that is NOT live"
    Never move a camera while it is the one shown on the livestream — viewers
    will see it swing around. Switch the livestream to the **other** camera
    first, then move this one. See
    [Camera Operation](camera-operation.md#dont-switch-while-moving).

---

## How to move a camera by hand

1. Press the **camera select** button for the camera you want to move
   (**Camera 1** or **Camera 2**).
2. Confirm that camera is **not** currently on the livestream.
3. Use the **joystick** to pan and tilt.
4. Use the **zoom** control to frame the shot.
5. When the shot looks good, **switch the livestream** to that camera (see
   [Camera Operation](camera-operation.md)).

!!! tip "Move gently"
    Small, slow movements look professional. Big, fast joystick moves look
    jerky on the livestream.

---

## How to move Camera 2 (worked example)

> "How do I move Camera 2?"

1. On the controller, press the **Camera 2** select button.
2. Make sure the livestream is currently showing **Camera 1** (so viewers
   don't see Camera 2 move).
3. Push the **joystick** to aim Camera 2 where you want.
4. Use the **zoom** to get the framing right (Camera 2 zooms up to 20×).
5. Switch the livestream to **Camera 2** when ready.

---

## Saving a preset (optional, advanced)

If you have found a shot you will reuse, you can save it as a preset:

1. Select the camera.
2. Aim and zoom it exactly how you want.
3. Hold/press the **set** (store) button, then a **number** button.

To recall it later, press that **number** button (or the matching StreamDeck
preset button).

!!! note "Check with Mills IT before changing existing presets"
    Recalling a preset is always safe. **Overwriting** an existing numbered
    preset changes it for everyone. If unsure, save to an unused number or ask
    Mills IT.
