# Turn System On

Start on the desk, and work accross. 

1. Turn on the PTZ controller (rocker switch back right, near top)
2. Turn on the Rode Caster Video (Red push button once, back right)
3. Turn on the A&H QU-5D Mixer (push button back right, near bottom power cable)
4. Turn on the computer (push button on front of mini PC in rack)
5. Turn on Foyer amp (switch on front of amp at front of rack)
6. Turn on small devices (back of rack, power switch on top power strip)
7. Turn on both speakers (light switches on back of rack on the floor)

# System checks

1. Ensure Lectern and Pulpit mics are unmuted and up between 20-5db, check that both mics are working and coming out the speakers
2. Ensure the choir mic is unmuted and up, look for volume coming through on multiview to ensure volume is coming up on livestream
3. With choir still up, check that foyer has volume from the choir

# Turn System Off

Exact reverse of turn on procedure:

1. Turn off both speakers (light switches on back of rack on the floor)
2. Turn off small devices (back of rack, power switch on top power strip)
3. Turn off Foyer amp (switch on front of amp at front of rack)
4. Turn off the A&H QU-5D Mixer (push button back right, near bottom power cable)
5. Turn off the Rode Caster Video (Red push button once, back right)
6. Turn off the PTZ controller (rocker switch back right, near top)
7. Shutdown the computer from the computer (push button on front of mini PC in rack)
